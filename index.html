Alexandra Marquez

Independent Storyteller & Politics Enthusiast

University of Maryland Class of 2021

I am a junior journalism and anthropology major at the University of Maryland, College Park. As a Silver Spring, Maryland, native I always dreamed of reporting in Washington, D.C. Last semester, I had the opportunity to do so at the McClatchy D.C. Bureau. I covered various congressional and national news for McClatchy, with a focus on covering the Texas congressional delegation for the Fort Worth Star-Telegram. I've previously written for The Diamondback in College Park and for Washington Gardener Magazine in Silver Spring.

My parents are from El Salvador and Ukraine and I speak three languages – Russian, Spanish and English. My passion for journalism began when I joined my high school newspaper and began covering issues important to the Silver Spring community, like Board of Education initiatives, new demographic trends in the school district and opportunity gaps between students of different socio-economic backgrounds. When I'm not reading, writing or listening to the news, I'm probably playing with my two cats, Lio and Leto.
